What is I4G all about..?

Our Vision

Our goal is to create and increase the earning power of one million African youths in tech through training and resources to help them impact and contribute to the development of Africa socially and economically.

Our Mission

Ingressive For Good is on a mission to empower African youths with tech skills to help increase their earning power. This is done through: Microscholarships, Tech Training and Talent Placement.
